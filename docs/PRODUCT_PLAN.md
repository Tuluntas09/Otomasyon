# PRODUCT_PLAN.md

## What Otomasyon is

Otomasyon is a **local-first personal finance research instrument** for a single user who
manages one portfolio and wants to understand its real risk shape — not to be told what to
do, but to see clearly what is.

## Who it is for

A single, technically literate user who:

- Holds a personal equity portfolio in USD.
- Wants to track concentration, drawdown, and volatility exposure without relying on a
  brokerage dashboard.
- Wants to record the reasoning behind their own decisions in a searchable journal.
- Wants rule-based, explainable alerts — not algorithmic trade signals.

## Positioning

This tool occupies the space between a spreadsheet and a brokerage dashboard: more
structured than a spreadsheet, more transparent than a black-box dashboard, and deliberately
without the ability to act on markets.

## Non-advisory stance

The system computes facts. Interpretations belong to the user. No output from this system
constitutes investment advice, a recommendation to buy or sell any security, or a prediction
of future performance.
